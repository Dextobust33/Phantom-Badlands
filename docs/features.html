Game Features

Combat & Classes

  • 9 playable classes across Warrior, Mage, and Trickster archetypes
  • Turn-based combat with abilities, buffs, and tactical decisions
  • Permadeath with Sanctuary system — companions, stored items, and Baddie Points survive
  • Companion system with 50+ monster types, egg hatching, and four fusion modes (Same Type, Mixed T9, Hybrid, Tier Ascension)
  • Elite Champion monsters with boosted rewards
  • Apex Frontier zones beyond 1500 tiles with named regions (Burning Reach, Frostbound Verge, Sundered Hollows, Cinder Wastes) — Apex variant monsters with boosted stats and +30% XP

World & Exploration

  • Procedurally generated world with 58 trading posts
  • 53 unique dungeons — every monster type has one, with Normal and Hard modes
  • Dynamic daily quests that scale to your level (no real-time cooldowns — re-run as fast as you like)
  • Repeatable quest chains at T1, T2, and T3 (13 chains total)
  • Threat HUD on the Area line — see spillover monster type, source dungeon, tile distance, and saturation count
  • Party system for up to 4 players with snake-formation movement

Crafting & Building

  • 5 crafting skills: Blacksmithing, Alchemy, Enchanting, Scribing, Construction
  • Recipe discovery through dungeon exploration
  • Tempered crafting — risk extra materials for guaranteed bonus stats
  • Gathering jobs: Mining, Logging, Fishing, Foraging, Soldier
  • 23 cosmetic player-buildable structures spanning Construction skill 3 → 25 — banners, lamp posts, statues, fountains, wells, hedges, mosaics, shrines, totems, and more
  • Player-built enclosures, bridges, guard posts, towers, and Companion Stables

Economy & Trade

  • Player market with Valor currency and supply/demand pricing
  • Specialty post discounts and threat-zone price hikes
  • Travel Stone consumables for buying remote market listings without traveling
  • Direct player-to-player trading (items, companions, eggs)
  • Account-level trade history (/trades) — rolling log of every market buy/sale and direct trade, persistent across permadeath
  • Curiosity Trader, Forge Master, Provisioner, and other NPC vendors at specialty posts

Multiplayer & Social

  • Real-time multiplayer with three color-coded chat channels: whisper, clan (/c), and party (/p)
  • Mentor system — Lv 20+ players can volunteer with /mentor on. Partying with a Lv < 10 player grants the whole party +25% XP on every kill
  • Friend list — account-level bidirectional friend graph (/friend add/list/requests) with online status, current character, and AFK badges. Friends survive permadeath
  • Block list — silently silence unwanted whispers (/block <user>); blocked users don't know they were blocked
  • Clan system — shared 30-slot vault, custom banner color, leader-set description and motto, online member dots, login/logout chat broadcasts, /clist roster
  • AFK status (/afk) with auto-clear on move or chat
  • Server-wide achievement announcements + Top-5 leaderboard alerts
  • Title system: Jarl, High King, Elder, Eternal — each with unique powers

New Player Experience

  • 12 first-touch tutorial overlays — modal popups teach you the progression dashboard, quest board, dungeons, crafting, signposts, Apex Frontier, market, chain quests, companions, the Companion Stable, gathering, and equipment when you first encounter them
  • Help discovery — /topics lists every help topic, /topic <key> opens any topic from anywhere; every panel has a ? Help button
  • [NEW Lv X] tag on whispers from Lv < 10 players helps mentors prioritize
  • Players list shows total online + active + AFK + mentor counts at a glance
  • Auto-updating launcher keeps you on the latest version
