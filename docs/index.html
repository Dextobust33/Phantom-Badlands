Phantom Badlands

A text-based multiplayer RPG where every death is permanent.

Explore a procedural world, master 9 classes, crawl dungeons, craft powerful gear, and build your legacy — one life at a time.

Download Free Learn More Join Discord

Permadeath

Every choice matters. Death is permanent, but your Sanctuary preserves companions and items across lives.

9 Classes

Warriors, Mages, and Tricksters — each with 3 specializations, unique abilities, and playstyles.

Procedural World

58 trading posts, dynamic quests, roaming merchants, and a world that scales from beginner to endgame.

53 Dungeons

Every monster type has its own dungeon. Clear them for guaranteed companion eggs. Unlock Hard Mode for greater rewards.

Deep Crafting

5 crafting skills, recipe discovery, tempered crafting, and a 23-structure cosmetic catalogue for shaping your settlement.

Multiplayer & Social

Parties up to 4, three chat channels (whisper / clan / party), friend list with online status, clan vault, and a mentor system that rewards veterans for guiding newcomers.

Mentor Bonus

Lv 20+ mentors who party with new players grant the whole party +25% XP on every kill. Mentoring isn't just flavor — it's a real reason to coordinate.

Apex Frontier

Beyond 1500 tiles lie four named zones — Burning Reach, Frostbound Verge, Sundered Hollows, Cinder Wastes — with elite Apex variants and bonus XP.
